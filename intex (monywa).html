Discover Monywa

The Heart of Upper Myanmar

Top Places to Visit

Thanboddhay Pagoda

One of Myanmar’s most colorful and unique temples with thousands of Buddha images.

Laykyun Sekkya Buddha

The second tallest Buddha statue in the world — a symbol of peace and devotion.

Local Culture & Traditions

Monywa is home to traditional Myanmar crafts, festivals, and the warm hospitality of its people. Visit during Thadingyut or Thingyan to see the city come alive.

Local Food

Shan Noodles

A popular dish you can find in many tea shops around Monywa.

Mohinga

Myanmar’s national dish — a must-try breakfast for every traveler!
